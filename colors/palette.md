# Modulbit color palette

## Primary colors
Main brand colors used for backgrounds and core UI

- Dark Base: #0A2E36
- Deep Teal: #0E3A40

## Accent Colors
Used for highlights, buttons and important elements.

- Neon Green: #27FB6B
- Soft Green: #14CC60

## Neutral Colors
Used for text, backgrounds and balance.

- White: #FFFFFF
- Light Gray: #F5F5F5
- Dark Gray: #1A1A1A

## Usage Guidelines

- Use dark colors as primary background
- Use accent green sparingly for emphasis
- Keep good contrast for readability
- Avoid overusing bright colors

---

## Example Usage

- Background: #0A2E36
- Text: #FFFFFF
- Highlight / Button: #27FB6B
